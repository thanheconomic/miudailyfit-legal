Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

MiuDailyFit collects minimal information necessary to operate the Service:

  • TikTok Account Data: Basic account information (username, user ID, display name) obtained through TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 authorization flow
  • Content Data: Video scripts, captions, and product information used to create TikTok content

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Authenticate and connect to your TikTok account via TikTok's Content Posting API
  • Post video content on your behalf after your explicit approval
  • Display your account information within the application interface

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

We store only the OAuth access token required for TikTok API authentication. This token is:

  • Stored securely in an encrypted local environment file
  • Never transmitted to external servers beyond TikTok's official API endpoints
  • Automatically invalidated when you revoke access through TikTok Settings

4. TikTok API & Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with TikTok's official APIs. Your use of TikTok through our Service is also subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy. We access only the permissions you explicitly grant during authorization:

  • user.info.basic — Read basic profile information
  • video.publish — Publish videos to your TikTok account
  • video.upload — Upload video files

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access at any time via TikTok Settings → Security → Authorized Apps → remove MiuDailyFit
  • Request deletion of any stored data by contacting us
  • Access information we hold about you upon request

6. Cookies

MiuDailyFit does not use cookies or tracking technologies on this website.

7. Children's Privacy

This Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact: thanheconomic@gmail.com
